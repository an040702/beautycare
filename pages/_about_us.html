OUR HISTORY

The early 1985, brothers John and Thomson, along with chemist CR Lachman, would change the beauty industry forever.Simplon began with a single product: revolutionary nail enamel that came in colors other than red.

1985
In 1985, our company was established with 12 original members, the original milestone was the outstanding products of the region.At the same time,Simplon partnered with some of the world’s most “unforgettable women,” including Iman, Claudia Schiffer, Cindy Crawford and Christy Turlington .
1990s
Simplon debuted its PerferStay™ collection in the 1990s. Featuring Brand Ambassador Halle Berry, the line soon became the longwear brand — and has remained so ever since .
2000s
The millennium brought a bold new influence to Simplon when world-renowned makeup artist Gucci Westman joined as Global Artist Director through March 2018, pushing the brand on the forefront of trend and fashion .
